Interactive Sound Installation

This is a demo of a prototype sound installation I began whilst studying at BIMM,
and have continued to work on post-graduation.

The idea was to experiment with alternative ways of creating music, in order to
make the process of creation more accessible to people without any musical knowledge

The 3 devices use sensors to convert human interactions into numerical values so
they can control and manipulate the sound and visuals being played.

I used Arduinos to digitise the sensor interactions, and did all the sound design in
MaxMSP.
